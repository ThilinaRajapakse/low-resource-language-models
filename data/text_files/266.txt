Tekinoronjĩ kũhunja
Tekinoronjĩ ya műűkano thutha wa guthugundwo ria mbere no muhaka ihunje nigetha itumirwo nia andu.
Thiori wa kũhunja kwa Tekinoronjĩ
Thiori cia kũhunja gwa Tekinoronjĩ ni nyingi imwe ya iria itumagirwo muno nĩ Rogers. Ũtuĩria wa Rogers wona kũhunja gwa Tekinoronjĩ cia műűkano űteithagiririo ni: wega wayo, Kurũtithania wira na iria ingi, kugereka, Kuhigicana na koneneka [1]
Thiori ĩno ya Rogers no ĩtũmĩrwo gũthondeka ciugo njerũ cia tekinoronjĩ cĩ kũhunja na ihenya. Kiugo kĩeru kĩgĩkorwo gĩkĩega gwĩ iria ciratũmirwo nĩgĩ gwĩtĩkĩrĩka nahenya.

Haria hagwete
1. Rogers Everett , Diffussion of innovations, the Free press 1995 page 15-16.
