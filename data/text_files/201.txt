Nigetha kwandika ũhoro wa philosophy no muhaka utumire tamunoronjĩ. tamunoronjtũmĩte iria tugutumira ni reality, ontology na epistemology. Kuruguria gwa tamunoronjĩ ici nita uu: Reality ni kĩĩhari kurigana na kiruguri kia gikuyu [1]. Ontology tũkũmita kiohari, epistemology tũmĩte mumenyerero na paradigm tũmĩte kĩambĩki/baragiyi. Ciothomoronjĩ tũmĩte cosmology
Philosophy itaguo atia na gikuyu. Haha tugeria guthondeka ritwa ria tugitumira C-K thiori Rītwa ria philosophy.
