Ũthuthuria wa SWOT
SWOT na Gĩthűgű nĩ Strengths, Weakness, Opportunities and Threats. Na gĩkűyű no ĩtwo űthuthuria wa gűtűmira űhoti, iremethia, mieke na iguoyohia.
