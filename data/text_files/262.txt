Ford
Kambuni ya USA ĩthondekaga ngari cia Ford. Kambuni ya njĩrĩrio nĩ műndű wetagwo Henry Ford.
Mass Production System (MPS)
Ford nĩ gundűririe műthodekere wa ngari witagwo MPS űria űthondekaga ngari nyingi ihanine. MPS nĩ tumirirwo ni iwanda cia gűthondeka indo ingĩ na iki hunja mabururi mangi.
