Ndwari thithitemu
Matemo
Ndwari thithitemu ni ikoragwo ĩĩ mananja wa handiwea. Ĩrutaga wira uyu igitumira gikundi kĩa programu kana sofutiwea.
Ndwari thithitemu ikoragwo thokoini
Ndwari thithimu igĩri ĩtumagirwo mũno komputaini cia PC nĩ Windows(Ndirisha) na Linux. Microsoft nĩ ĩrekerie thokoini ndwari thithitemu ya Windows 8 (Ndirisha 98) mweriinĩ wa Octomber 2012.
ĩturanĩro cia Ndwari thithitemu Ndwari thithitemu nikoragwo na ĩturanĩro nyingĩ ta Driver/s (Terevi), Kernel (ngorono).
