Ũthuthuria wa Patent
Patent ĩhenyanagwo thutha wa mundu guthunda kĩndu kieru na kűria kĩama kĩa bűrűri wake, bűrűri űngĩ, kĩama kĩa patent kia thĩ kuheo patent.

Ciuragio kuma ciama iria ihenaga patent ta kia thĩ kia WIPO.
Kwi ũtuĩria wĩkĩtwo wa gűthuthuria patent iria cia na henyano mĩaka mingĩ műthiru.
Kwi patent nyingi ugithoroma rurendaini.

Kambuni nVkoretwo ĩgĩtagana hĩndi ĩrie patent ciao ciatumĩrwe matahenyanĩte rutha. cira wa ica ikuhĩ nĩ gatagatĩ ka Samsung na Apple. Igoti ria USA riaguire Kambuni ya Samsung nĩ ya huthire uru pantent cia Apple. Igoti rĩa Korea rĩaguire Kambuni ya Samsung na Apple nĩ ciahuthire uru pantent cia kabuni ĩyo ĩngĩ.
