Kũgerithimithia Meciria
Kũgerithimithia kwa gũtumira Meciria nĩ thought experiment. Kũgerithimithia gute gutumagira miro, kigererio kana githimiro (Laboratory). Kũgerithimithia Meciria gutumarigirwo hindi gutari miro igihota gutumirwo Kũgerithimithia.
Kũgerithimithia Meciria nĩ ĩgera itũmagĩrwo kũhura mbica cia meciria nĩgetha gũtuaria nduire, gutumagirwo fithikiinĩ, ucuumiinĩ, mathabuinĩ na firofĩinĩ[1].
