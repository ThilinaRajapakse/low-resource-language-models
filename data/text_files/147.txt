Injiniarini ya Okioharĩ
Injiniarini ya Okioharĩ nĩ Ontological engineering. Injiniarini mũthemba ũyũ ĩthondekagũo na mathũgundo hamwe na ũria mathũgundo mataraine.
Kwĩ kĩohari thondeke nyingĩ cia Injiniarini ta Sowa kĩohari, kĩoburemu na ingĩ. kĩoburemu nĩ kĩoharĩ ya gũthondeka njira cia gwaka sofutiwea. kĩoburemu ithodeketwo na kĩohari nyingĩ ĩmwe ya cio nĩ kĩohari ya ũjumuisho.
kĩohari ya ũjumuisho nyonanite ati Kĩĩhari thondekagwa nĩ ibandi cia ĩundu, Buremuwaki ya ũtuĩria ĩthondeketwo na haria andũ mararore [1].
