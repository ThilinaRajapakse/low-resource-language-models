Gũtaarĩria Marũa ma Keerĩ ma Johana maandĩkirwo nĩ “Mũthuuri” akĩandĩkĩra “Mũtumia ũrĩa wendeetwo hamwe na ciana ciake.” Ũguo hiihi nĩkuuga atĩ aandĩkagĩra kanitha hamwe na andũ aayo. Marũa maya makuhĩ maheanaga ũrĩa andũ maagĩrĩirwo nĩkwendana mũndũ na ũrĩa ũngĩ, na ũgwati wa arutani a maheeni na moorutani maao.
