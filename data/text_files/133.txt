Ronjiki ihuthikaga kurepresenti norenji ya macini.
Kwĩ ronjiki mithemba mĩngĩ ta:
1. Boolean Logic
kuga niguo kana tiguo
2. Proposition Logic
uria indo ciitainwo. kwa muhiano. Kamau e mbere ya njeri
Ihuthagira alama cia conjuction (and) na disjuction (0r)
3. Predicate Logic
Ronjiki ino ituraninagira indo ciothe cia seti furani. Kwa muhiano Ngui ciothe cii maguru mana
ihuthagira arama cia existential na universal
4. Fuzzy Logic
Ino ni ronjiki ya kunina uncertainities thinii wa norenji
